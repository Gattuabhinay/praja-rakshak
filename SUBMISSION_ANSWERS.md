## Theme & Problem Statement
Product wrap: **PrajaSetu = Clean Up + Speak Up** for Telangana.

Problem: Citizens face unclean surroundings from wrong waste segregation/dumping, and blocked civic justice when police demand bribes, refuse FIRs, or officials seek money. Many do not know what to write or which portal to use.

Approach: Clean Up uses vision AI to classify waste from a photo. Speak Up uses AI to draft complaints for waste, bribery, FIR refusal, corruption, and social/legal cases, then redirects to official portals (GHMC, TSPCB, ACB, Lokayukta, Police, CPGRAMS). AI prepares; government systems file.

## Solution Description
PrajaSetu is one social-impact web platform with two clear paths:
1) Clean Up — multimodal vision AI classifies Wet/Dry/Hazardous/E-waste with tips.
2) Speak Up — AI drafts a formal complaint by case type and opens the matching official portal.

AI methods: Gemini Vision classification + Gemini complaint drafting + local vision fallback.  
Stack: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Google Generative AI, Vercel.
